The Mazda brand is one of the most know in the world for his sports cars, one of the most know is the Mazda RX-7, for his incredible speed and characteristics of the face in the car.

The Mazda Rx-7 is a lightweight sports car that was producede form 1978 to 2002. It is know for its unique rotary engine, which provides a high power-to-weight ratio and exceptional handling. The RX-7 features a sleek and aerodynamic design, with a long hood and short rear deck. It is available in various trims, including the turbocharged models that offers even more performance.The car's interior is drive-focused, with supportive seats and modern technology. Overall, the Mazda RX-7 is celebrated for its agility, and thrilling driving experience.

the components of this car are:

  • Engine: 1.3L twin-rotor Wankel
  • Power: 276HP
  • Torque: 98.43kg
  • Transmission: 5-speed manual
  • 0-100 km/h: 5.9 seconds
  • Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive
